Collins Custom Interiors

Established in 1960, Collins Custom Interiors has built a reputation over the years for exceptional quality and personalized designs, Jackie Collins first opened Collins Custom Interiors in Hurst, Tx. on the corner of Anderson Dr. and Old 183.

People are always asking, “Rick, how long have you been doing upholstery work?” Rick replies, “How do I answer that? I have been going to work with my father since I was 3-4 years old. I have literally been doing this most my life.”

In 1984, Rick Collins officially took over the business for his father honoring the tradition of custom upholstery craftsmanship. In April of 1988 they moved to their present location in Colleyville where they strive for excellence in all of the projects they handle.

For more information about our custom upholstery services, please don’t hesitate to contact us. We would be more than happy to consult with you and discuss how we can help you achieve a fresh look for any project.
